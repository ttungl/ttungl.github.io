I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at The Center for Advanced Computer Studies (CACS), University of Louisiana at Lafayette. My co-advisors are Prof. Danella Zhao and Prof. Magdy A. Bayoumi. I am interested in networks, cloud computing, and areas in between. My current research projects are on the heterogeneous exascale manycore systems. A common theme in my research is the use of optimization techniques and efficient algorithms to optimize the network-on-chip scalability, energy-efficient, and high-performance computing on-Chip Multiple Processors. Previously, I was a research assistant at Networked Systems Lab., and received my Master degree in IT Convergence Engineering, School of Electronic Engineering, Kumoh National Institute of Technology, South Korea.
